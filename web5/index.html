My Personal Journey

A personal value I would like to share with the world


My hope is for the next generation of people to be able to read my journal and understand that I fought my entire career to become who I am today. I value a world where regardless of color or sexuality everyone has the same chance. My goal is to break down barriers keeping people of color and women out of tech.

The money I was making as a security professional/ Account manager was... shall we say.. REDICULOUS but I was still very unhappy

I found my calling during covid when I personally was able to help out my community by provided 35 laptops to students at Mcclain and Central free of charge. I realized my talents were needed elswhere.

In my career I have been:

  • successful
  • homeless
  • discriminated> against
  • arrested for doing my job
  • Revered by colleagues
  • Lonely
  • Loved
  • Designing a Non-Profit to provide Cyber Security Services to minority ownend businesses

I do what I do now for my community, my people, and my friends that never made it out of the hood

Jeremy Stevenson
